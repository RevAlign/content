### email_infra.png
**Title:** Email Infrastructure That Actually Works

Your cold emails land in spam because you skipped the boring stuff.

**The 5 Layers of Cold Email Infrastructure:**

1. **Domains** - Never send from your main domain. Buy 3-5 secondary domains. $10-12/year each.

2. **DNS Records** - SPF, DKIM, DMARC. If these acronyms mean nothing, your emails mean nothing to inbox providers.

3. **Sending Infrastructure** - Mailbox rotation, IP reputation, send limits. This is where Instantly/Smartlead earn their fee.

4. **Warmup** - 2-4 weeks of simulated engagement before you send a single cold email. Skip this and you're DOA.

5. **Validation** - ZeroBounce or NeverBounce every list. One hard bounce tanks your sender score.

**The Order Matters:**
Domains → DNS → Infrastructure → Warmup → Validate → Then send.

Most people start at step 5 and wonder why they're in spam.

Which layer did you skip?
